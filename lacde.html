Pre-requisite: None

Course Outcomes: At the end of the course, the student will be able to:

  1. Understand the concepts of vector space and linear transformations
  2. Apply orthogonal and congruent transformations to a quadratic form.
  3. Apply the concepts in solving physical problems arising in engineering
  4. Find the maxima and minima of multivariable functions
