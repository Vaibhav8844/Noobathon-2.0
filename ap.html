         APPLIED PHYSICS                                               CREDIT:3

Pre-requisite: None

Course Outcomes: At the end of the course, the student will be able to:

  1. Understand the applications of Interference, diffraction, optical fibers, holography and lasers in engineering
  2. Understand the basics of semiconductors, magnetism, super conductivity, nano materials and their applications in engineering.
  3. Comprehend sensing technologies and their applications in computer science and engineering
